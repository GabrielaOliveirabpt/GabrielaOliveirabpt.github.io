About me


I am a Brazilian web developer, illustrator and translator. I graduated in Fine Arts in 2004 and worked as an Arts Teacher for kids for 4 years. I have always loved languages, I speak Portuguese, English, Spanish, French and a little bit of German. I lived in Ireland for 5 years where I took a Master in Translation Studies. There, I worked as a Language Quality Assurance Tester for a Localization company on the video games field. I also worked as a Data Editor for Apple in the Maps application. I moved to Berlin in 2017, where I am currently learning German and how to code.
